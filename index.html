Wake up to the water, breathe in the aloha

From the moment you open the door, the ocean greets you. This is the spirit of aloha — and it's waiting to welcome you home.

The Space

Your home away from home

This is no cookie-cutter hotel room — it's a hale with heart. Newly renovated and thoughtfully appointed, Hale ʻIke Ilikai welcomes you with a full kitchen of granite countertops and new cabinetry, stocked with everything you need to cook a meal and gather your ʻohana around the table.

Everywhere you look, the islands shine through: authentic Hawaiian artwork and handcrafted Koa wood furniture, made with care by a local Hawaiian artist. It's a sense of place — and a sense of welcome — you simply won't find in a standard rental.

The condo comfortably sleeps four: a queen bed in the bedroom and a sofa bed in the living room, with one full bath. Fast WiFi keeps you connected — though with a view like this, you may forget to check your phone.

The Location

The heart of Waikiki at your doorstep

The Ilikai Marina is one of Waikiki's most prized and secure addresses — toes-in-the-sand close to the beach, with a private elevated walkway leading straight to The Modern's waterfront restaurant and cocktail lounge. You'll even have direct views of the world-renowned South Shore surf breaks — Ala Moana Bowls ("Bowls") and Kaisers — right from your windows. Everything you came to Hawaiʻi for is just steps away.

Things to do nearby

  • World-renowned surf breaks in view — watch the surfers carve Ala Moana Bowls ("Bowls") and Kaisers right from your lanai
  • Hilton Hawaiian Village Lagoon — calm, protected water for swimming, just steps away
  • Waikiki Beach — world-famous sand and surf, ~5-minute walk
  • Ala Moana Center — one of the world's largest open-air malls
  • Hawaii Convention Center — an easy walk from your door
  • Saturday morning farmers' market — local produce, coffee, and treats
  • The Modern — excellent dining right next door

Getting here

  • About a 15–20 minute drive from Honolulu International Airport (HNL)
  • The airport express bus drops off right out front of the Ilikai
  • On-site parking under the building, $35/day, subject to availability
  • Convenience stores across the street and next door at the Ilikai Hotel
