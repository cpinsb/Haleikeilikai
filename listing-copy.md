# Hale ʻIke Ilikai — Oceanfront Corner Suite with 180° Waikiki Views

Wake up to the water. This 15th-floor corner unit at the Ilikai Marina puts the ocean in every room, with a sweeping 180-degree view that runs from the Hilton Hawaiian Village Lagoon all the way to Magic Island and downtown Honolulu. The Yacht Harbor's edge sits just 30 feet below your windows — close enough to watch the boats come and go with your morning coffee. And every evening, the sky puts on a show: front-row sunset views melting into the ocean, right from your lanai — followed on Friday nights by the Hilton Hawaiian Village fireworks bursting over the lagoon, all from the comfort of your own balcony.

The Ilikai Marina is one of Waikiki's most prized and secure addresses. You're steps from the beach, and a private elevated walkway connects you directly to The Modern, home to a stunning waterfront restaurant and cocktail lounge.

Guests agree: Hale ʻIke Ilikai is rated **4.76★ across 152 stays**, with near-perfect marks for location, check-in, and communication.

## The Space

Newly renovated and thoughtfully appointed, Hale ʻIke Ilikai is a home, not a hotel room. The full kitchen features granite countertops and new cabinetry, fully stocked with pots, pans, dishes, and utensils so you can cook and dine in whenever you like. Throughout the unit you'll find authentic Hawaiian artwork and handcrafted Koa wood furniture, made locally by a Hawaiian artist — a real sense of place you won't get in a standard rental.

The condo comfortably sleeps four: a queen bed in the bedroom and a sofa bed in the living room, with one full bath. Fast WiFi keeps you connected, whether you're working remotely or just sharing the view.

## Good to Know Before You Book

This is a privately owned, self-service condo — which is part of what makes it feel like your own Hawaiian home rather than a hotel. A few things to plan around:

- **Check-in:** After 3:00 PM. Early check-in is sometimes available depending on the cleaning schedule — just ask.
- **Check-out:** 11:00 AM. Our cleaners need prompt access to prep for the next guests, so we're unable to offer late check-out. A $30 fee applies to departures after 11:00 AM, since belongings left behind require us to reschedule cleaning.
- **Self-service stay:** There's no daily housekeeping or linen/towel service. Additional cleaning can be arranged for a fee — just let us know. Coin-operated washers and dryers are on the 8th floor.
- **Self check-in:** Let yourself in with the keypad — no front desk, no waiting in line. Access details are sent within 24 hours of arrival; we hold them until then for your security and ours.
- **Parking:** Available on-site under the building, subject to availability, at $35/day — arranged directly with the parking attendant.
- **Mail & packages:** For liability reasons, we're unable to receive mail or deliveries at the condo.
